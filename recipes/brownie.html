Best Brownie Recipe Ever

Description:

These are one of my favorite desserts to make. I spend many rainy afternoon with the family making these delicious brownies and now store bought just wont do.

This simple and quick recipe will be sure to brighten up your families day and impress party guests of any kind. Lets get started!

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups of granulated sugar.
  • 3/4 cup all purpose flour.
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder, sift if lumpy.
  • 1/2 cup of powdered sugar, sift if lumpy.
  • 1/2 cup of dark chocolate chips.
  • 3/4 tsp of sea salt.
  • 2 large eggs.
  • 1/2 cup of canola oil or extra virgin olive oil.
  • 2 tablespoons of water.
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla.

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F. Lightly spray an 8x8 baking dish(ot a 9x9 or the brownies will overcook. with cooking spray and line it with parchment paper. Spray the paper afterwards.
  2. In a medium bowl combine the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, oiive oil, water, and vanilla.
  4. Sprinkle the dry mix over the wet mix and stir until combined.
  5. Pour the bater into the prepared pan and use a spatula to smooth the top. Bake for 40 to 48 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out with only a few crumbs attached. Cool completely before slicing. Store in an airtight container for upto 3 days. They can also be frozen!
